ABOUT SPARDHA '25

Spardha, JKLU's annual Sports Festival is a three-day event. The gala occasion is witness to an eclectic display of sportsmanship and team spirit where students from across the country come together to participate in sports competitions held on campus.

Since 2013, Spardha is celebrated annually. The three-day event oversees a number of outdoor and indoor sports competitions that include cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, kabaddi, cross fit, and athletics held on the campus that bring out the sportsmanship and team spirit of the students. We will have students from over 30 colleges, participating in 7 different sporting events.

This year's Spardha promises to be the biggest yet, with a spectacular lineup of sports and activities for participants from all over India. In addition to the competitive tournaments, Spardha will also feature a range of cultural activities, including live music and dance performances, as well as a variety of fun games and activities for the whole family to enjoy.

Date: 21-23 February, 2025.

Venue: JK Lakshmipat University, Near Mahindra SEZ, P.O. Mahapura Ajmer Road, Jaipur – 302 026, Rajasthan, India.
